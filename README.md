## 3. Getting Started

### 3.1 Account Registration

1. Open the **HAPSAY360** application.
2. Tap **Sign Up** on the login screen.
3. Complete the registration form:

   * Given Name
   * Middle Name
   * Surname
   * Email Address
   * Password & Confirm Password
   * Phone Number (optional but recommended)
4. Tap **Create Account**.
5. Upon successful registration, you will be redirected to the login screen.

### 3.2 Logging In

1. Enter your registered email address.
2. Enter your password.
3. Tap **Log In**.
4. Allow location access when prompted.

### 3.3 Password Recovery

1. Tap **Forgot Your Password?**
2. Enter your registered email address.
3. Enter the One-Time Password (OTP) sent to your email.
4. Create and confirm a new password.

## 6. Police Services

### 6.1 Filing a Police Blotter

A police blotter is an official record of an incident reported to the police.

**Procedure:**

1. Tap **File Blotter** on the Home Screen.
2. Review and confirm personal information.
3. Provide incident details:

   * Incident type
   * Date and time
   * Location (via map)
   * Description
4. Attach supporting evidence (photos, videos, or documents), if available.
5. Submit the report.

ğŸ“„ A **blotter reference number** will be issued for tracking.

### 6.2 Tracking Blotter Requests

1. Tap **Track Activity** on the Home Screen.
2. View submitted blotters and their status:

   * Pending
   * Completed
   * Rejected
3. Tap **Check Details** to view the full report.
