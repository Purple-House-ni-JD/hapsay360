1. Introduction
1.1 About HAPSAY360
HAPSAY360 is a mobile safety and police service application designed to connect citizens with Philippine National Police (PNP) services. The application enables users to request emergency assistance, locate nearby police stations, file police blotters, and apply for police clearance through a secure and user-friendly mobile platform.
1.2 Key Features
Emergency SOS alerts with real-time location sharing
Police blotter filing and tracking
Police clearance appointment booking
Nearest police station locator
Secure account and payment management
2. System Requirements
Supported Platforms: Android and iOS
Internet Connection: Required for all services
Required Permissions:
Location services
Camera (photo and video evidence)
Photo library access
Notifications
3. Getting Started
3.1 Account Registration
Open the HAPSAY360 application.
Tap Sign Up on the login screen.
Complete the registration form with the required information:
Given Name
Middle Name
Surname
Email Address
Password and Confirm Password
Phone Number (optional but recommended)
Tap Create Account.
Upon successful registration, you will be redirected to the login screen.
3.2 Logging In
Enter your registered email address.
Enter your password.
Tap Log In.
Allow location access when prompted.
3.3 Password Recovery
Tap Forgot Your Password?
Enter your registered email address.
Enter the One-Time Password (OTP) sent to your email.
Create and confirm a new password.
4. Application Overview
4.1 Home Screen
The Home Screen provides quick access to all major features of the application:
Book Appointment – Schedule police clearance appointments
Track Activity – Monitor blotter and request status
File Blotter – Submit police incident reports
Nearest Help – Locate nearby police stations
SOS Button – Emergency assistance
Announcements and advisories from police authorities are also displayed on the Home Screen.
5. Emergency Services
5.1 SOS Emergency Feature
Use only in real emergency situations.
To activate SOS:
Press and hold the red SOS button on the Home Screen for three (3) seconds.
Release early to cancel the request.
Once activated:
Your real-time GPS location is captured.
An alert is sent to the nearest police station.
Nearby stations and distances are displayed.
5.2 Nearest Help Locator
Tap Nearest Help on the Home Screen.
View nearby police stations on the interactive map.
Select a station to:
Call directly
Send a message
Get directions via Google Maps
View detailed station information
6. Police Services
6.1 Filing a Police Blotter
A police blotter is an official record of an incident reported to the police.
Procedure:
Tap File Blotter on the Home Screen.
Review and confirm your personal information.
Provide incident details including:
Incident type
Date and time
Location (via map)
Description of the incident
Attach supporting evidence (photos, videos, or documents), if available.
Submit the report.
Upon submission, a blotter reference number is issued for tracking purposes.
6.2 Tracking Blotter Requests
Tap Track Activity on the Home Screen.
View submitted blotters with their current status:
Pending
Completed
Rejected
Tap Check Details to view full report information.
7. Police Clearance Application
7.1 Overview
Police Clearance is an official certification stating that an individual has no criminal record. Clearance is valid for six (6) months from the date of issuance.
7.2 Booking an Appointment
Tap Book Appointment on the Home Screen.
Review and accept the Terms and Conditions.
Complete or update your personal profile.
Select:
Purpose of clearance
Preferred police station
Available date and time
Confirm the appointment.
7.3 Payment and Verification
Clearance fee: ₱250.00
Select a payment method (Card, GCash, PayMaya, or Cash).
Upload payment proof if required.
Wait for booking confirmation.
8. Appointments & Payments
8.1 Viewing Appointments
Go to Profile → My Appointments.
View appointment details including date, time, status, and reference number.
8.2 Managing Payment Methods
Go to Profile → Payment.
Add, delete, or set default payment methods.
All payment information is encrypted for security.
9. Account & Profile Management
9.1 Editing Profile Information
Go to Profile → My Account.
Tap Edit Profile.
Update allowed fields and save changes.
9.2 Managing Addresses
Go to Profile → Addresses.
Edit and update address details as needed.
9.3 Password Management
Go to My Account → Change Password.
Enter your old password and set a new one.
10. Important Reminders
Always bring a valid government-issued ID for police clearance appointments.
Arrive at least 15 minutes before your scheduled appointment.
Emergency features should only be used in real emergencies.
Keep your profile information updated for accurate records.
